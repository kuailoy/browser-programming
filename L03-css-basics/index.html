About Me

I have several years of professional front-end development experience using TypeScript and JavaScript. Over time, I have contributed to the development of large-scale React applications used in real-world production environments. My responsibilities have ranged from implementing complex UI features to ensuring code quality, maintainability, and long-term scalability of the systems I work on.

In addition to feature development, I focus heavily on performance optimization and technical improvements. I have experience analyzing rendering bottlenecks, reducing bundle sizes, and improving application responsiveness across different devices and network conditions. I have also participated in refactoring legacy systems, simplifying overly complex logic, and introducing better architectural patterns to make codebases easier to understand and extend. Improving CI/CD workflows is another area I have worked on, helping teams achieve faster feedback cycles and more reliable deployments.

Beyond daily development tasks, I genuinely enjoy learning new tools, frameworks, and engineering practices. I regularly explore modern front-end technologies, keep up with industry trends, and look for opportunities to apply better solutions to existing problems. Continuous learning and self-improvement are important to me, as I believe strong engineering skills come from both hands-on experience and an openness to new ideas.
